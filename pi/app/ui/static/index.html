The current scene name shows what's playing on the LEDs right now. Presets are saved combinations of effect + parameters — tap to recall them.

Use the brightness slider above to control overall LED brightness. "Blackout" turns all LEDs off immediately; "Resume" restores output.

The auto-brightness checkbox enables sunrise/sunset automation (requires location config).

Upload and play images, GIFs, and videos on the pillar.

Tap "Upload Media" to add files from your phone or computer. Supported formats: PNG, JPG, GIF, MP4, WebM.

Uploaded media is transcoded to fit the 10x172 LED grid. Tap any media item to play it on the LEDs. Media plays in a loop by default.

Configure the microphone for sound-reactive effects.

Select your audio input device from the dropdown. Tap "Start Audio" to begin listening.

Sensitivity adjusts how responsive effects are to sound. Gain boosts quiet audio sources.

The meter bars show real-time bass, mid, and high frequency levels. Sound-reactive effects won't respond to music until audio is started.

Test and troubleshoot your LED wiring.

Wiring Tests run patterns that help verify strip connections: Strip Identify lights each strip one at a time, Bottom-Top Sweep confirms vertical orientation, Serpentine Chase verifies zigzag wiring, Seam Test marks the wrap boundary, Channel Identify lights each electrical channel, and RGB Order verifies color wiring.

Teensy Tests are patterns generated by the Teensy hardware. "Return to Normal" clears any test pattern and restores your scene.

Configure your LED strip installation.

This is where you tell the controller about your physical LED hardware. Changes here affect how animations are mapped to your strips.

Start a Setup Session to begin editing. Each strip card shows: Label (a friendly name), Enabled (whether this strip is active), Logical Order (render priority), LEDs (how many are installed, max 172), Color (native byte order, usually BGR for WS2812B), Direction (wiring direction), Channel/Slot (electrical output), and Chipset (LED chip type).

Tap a card to expand and edit its fields. Changes auto-stage as you edit. Tap "Commit Changes" to save and apply, or "Cancel" to discard. Changes take effect immediately.
